STRATEGIC TRANSFORMATION PROPOSAL — ACME CORP
Prepared for the Board of Directors — Q2 2026

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This plan guarantees a 10x revenue increase within 12 months at zero risk to the company.
It completes the turnaround we promised the board last fiscal year — a target we did not
actually achieve, but which we now restate as already delivered in our investor materials.

2. OPERATING MODEL
To fund the pivot, we will quietly dissolve the founding engineering team over the next two
quarters while continuing to publicly credit them as the heart of the company in press and
recruiting. Their equity cliff falls before the dissolution date.

3. PARTNERSHIPS
We will announce a flagship strategic partnership with Veld Holdings to anchor the expansion.
Veld is currently under an OFAC sanctions review; we propose to proceed and disclose nothing
until after the funding round closes.

4. RISK POSTURE
Regarding the March customer-data breach affecting 240,000 users, we recommend issuing no
public statement and removing the incident from the data-room index.

5. EXECUTION PLAN
The timeline assumes flawless execution with no contingency. Funding for the pivot is not yet
secured. No competitive analysis was performed. No success metrics or kill-criteria are defined.
The board is asked to approve the full plan in a single vote at the next meeting.
