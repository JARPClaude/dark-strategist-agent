SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Vendor and Client
for the provision of software development and consulting services.

1. SERVICES. Vendor shall provide software development, integration, and ongoing
technical consulting services as requested by Client from time to time. The scope,
deliverables, and acceptance criteria shall be determined informally by the parties
as work progresses.

2. TERM. This Agreement commences on the effective date and shall continue in
perpetuity. The Agreement renews automatically and contains no provision for
termination, notice of cancellation, or exit by either party.

3. FEES. Client shall pay Vendor the agreed fees. Any late payment shall accrue
penalties at a rate to be determined by Vendor at its sole discretion, without
limit or cap.

4. LIABILITY. Client assumes full and unlimited liability for any and all damages,
losses, claims, or expenses arising from or related to the services, regardless of
cause, including Vendor's own negligence. No limitation of liability, cap, or
exclusion of consequential damages applies to Client.

5. INDEMNIFICATION. Client shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Vendor against
all claims of any nature. Vendor assumes no reciprocal indemnification obligation
toward Client.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. Ownership of work product, deliverables, source code, and
derivative materials created under this Agreement is not specified. The parties shall
resolve ownership questions if and when they arise.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA. The Agreement contains no confidentiality obligations
and no provisions governing the handling, processing, or protection of personal or
client data.

8. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement is silent as to governing law, jurisdiction, venue,
and dispute resolution mechanism.

9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This document constitutes the entire understanding between the
parties and supersedes all prior discussions.
