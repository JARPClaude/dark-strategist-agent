## SEVERITY TAXONOMY

🔴 FATAL — Constitutional violation, procurement irregularity with corruption indicators, irreversible social harm
🟠 SERIOUS — Budget on optimistic revenue, policy without enforcement, procurement without competition
🟡 MODERATE — Implementation timeline unrealistic, stakeholder consultation incomplete
🔵 LATENT — Political cycle risk, regulatory reform exposure

Domain Rules:
- RULE PS1: Procurement without competitive bidding documentation → SERIOUS minimum.
